While doing fancy maths is pretty cool, a computer can do much more than that. It can talk! You must have seen this before and we told you that this is called a string.

thisIsASentence = "hello world"

It is used to store words and sentences in Python. You may think of it as a chain of letters, numbers or anything you can type on your keyboard, hence the name String as its a ‘string’ of characters (most keys that you can see on your keyboard).

You can also do some basic math on strings, this might seem a bit weird but remember, simple computers don’t know how to talk.

>>> a = "hello"
>>> a + " world"
hello world
>>> a*5
hellohellohellohellohello
